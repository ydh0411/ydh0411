### About

I am an undergraduate researcher working around diffusion MRI and medical image analysis. Right now I am learning how to turn imaging data, model code, and experiment notes into workflows that are easier to inspect, reproduce, and improve.

My interests sit between medical imaging, deep learning, and hands-on engineering. I like projects where the result has to work outside a notebook: a clean report pipeline, an embedded sensing system, or a data-processing script that someone else can actually run.

### Research Interests

`Diffusion MRI` `Microstructure Imaging` `Medical Image Analysis` `Deep Learning` `Reproducible Workflows`

- Diffusion MRI signal modeling and microstructure-related imaging.
- Deep learning for anatomical brain parcellation and medical image analysis.
- Practical tooling for lab reports, data processing, and research notes.
